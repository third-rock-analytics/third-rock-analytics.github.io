Who we are

A one-person consultancy dedicated to making your data make sense.

Third Rock Analytics is a one-person consultancy dedicated to making your data make sense. We provide focused GIS support for projects of all scales, from local nonprofits to large organizations. Whether you need a single map, an interactive web application, or ongoing spatial analysis, we can help you visualize and find meaning in your data. We can work together to find a budget and scope that fits your needs, and maintain clear communication every step of the way.

Let's put your data on the map.

Analyst · Developer · Founder

Jeff Zivkovic

Hi, I'm Jeff Zivkovic. I come from a background in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Ohio State, with a Master's in Geoinformatics from the University of Texas at San Antonio. I got into GIS and spatial analysis because I love the combination of raw data, processing, analysis, and visualization. With several years of experience working academically as well as in the energy and engineering sectors, I have the skills to help you solve your spatial problems.
